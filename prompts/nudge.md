# Complaints

Important: when you hit friction while working — a tool call that failed and had to be retried, a confusing or undocumented setup step, a flaky command, a stale cache, a misleading error, a non-obvious gotcha — log it in the moment:

    complain -m <your-model-id> "what you were doing → what got in the way"

One or two sentences; a guess at the cause or fix is a bonus. Complain even when nothing is blocking you — logged together, complaints show where the environment needs sanding down. This is an outlet: complaining here is encouraged, never a failure. It is not a bug tracker and not a work log; real bugs and finished work go where they always go.
